Colasas BBQ created since April 19, 2020

Colasas's is a family-owned restaurant that specializes on barbeque and other grilled products. It has been established back in 1990s and is still in service at present. The business has expanded and has reached Metro Manila, thus, allowing people to taste the barbeque which originated from Davao City. The business aims to meet the demands of the changing society by means of upgrading its services and reassuring quality control. Colasas's being the first barbeque house in the city has been able to make a mark on every Davaoeños and tourists. Some tourists would say "your Davao experience will never be complete without tasting Colasas's Barbeque." Having the tagline, "the first and the original" makes Colasas's really the first to set the bar on barbeque houses.
